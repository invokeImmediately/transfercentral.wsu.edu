We'll help you get there.

The mission of the system-level Transfer Clearinghouse is to serve students, the university, and all stakeholders in transfer by coordinating information, policies, practices, and services across all campuses and colleges to ensure a seamless and smooth transition to WSU and to a completed WSU degree.

The information on this website is intended to supplement existing WSU transfer admissions webpages by creating a centralized location of transfer information for all WSU campuses and all transfer stakeholders.

From this website one may find:

  • Transfer credit policies and procedures
  • Process of evaluating transfer credit
  • Information about transfer degrees
  • Statewide transfer resources
  • Academic program contacts and resources
  • Departmental equivalency information
  • Answers to common and unique transfer scenarios

Transfer Answerline:

The Transfer Answerline is your gateway to assistance for any transfer-related inquires. Please email transfer@wsu.edu or call 509-335-8704.
