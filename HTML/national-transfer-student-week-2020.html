The purpose of National Transfer Student Week is to raise the profile of transfer students, offer them support, and acknowledge what they bring to WSU! The Transfer Clearinghouse is excited to partner with others on campus to provide programming for our transfer students! Check out the events, student profiles and email/Zoom links below.
