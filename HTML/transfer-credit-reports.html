TITLE: Transfer Credit Reports URL: /transfer-credit-reports/

The Transfer Credit Report is used to display the evaluation of your transfer credit to WSU. It is displayed at the time of an admissions decision for prospective students, and it is updated soon after any new transfer credit is received by the Office of Admissions for prospective and current students.

If you discover any discrepancies with a transcript or new information becomes available regarding the evaluation of a course, please email transfer@wsu.edu to request an updated TCR.

How to View a Transfer Credit Report

  1. Log in to myWSU and go to My Student Center.
  2. Under Academics, select Transfer Credit Report from the drop down menu. Click on the double arrows to view.
  3. The top of the Transfer Credit Report will show a summary of transfer hours and a transfer GPA used for Admissions purposes.
  4. If an external degree was earned it will also appear on the Transfer Credit Report.
  5. Below the, you will see a table showing equivalencies for courses taken at institutions other than WSU. The transfer equivalency, credits, grade, and any UCORE fulfillment are provided in the table.
  6. Test Credit and Other Credits are located near the bottom of the page.
